Iliad 2: 864-865

From the Venetus A MS

μῄοσιν αῦ, Μέσθλης τε καὶ Ἄντιφος ἡγησάσθην

υἷε Ταλαιμένεος, τὼ τῶ γυγαίῃ τέκε λίμνη

And the Maeonians had captains twain, Mesthles and Antiphus, the two sons of TaIaemenes, whose mother was the nymph of the Gygaean lake; and they led the Maeonians, whose birth was beneath Tmolas.

A. T. Murray (1924)
